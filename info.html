India welcomes millions of international travelers every year for tourism, business, medical treatment, conferences, and other short-term visits. The India e-Visa system allows eligible foreign nationals to apply online without visiting an Indian Embassy or Consulate in many cases.

India e-Visa Online provides professional application assistance to help travelers complete their India e-Visa applications accurately and efficiently.

Important: India e-Visa Online is a private visa assistance service and is not affiliated with the Government of India. Final approval, processing time, and visa validity are determined solely by the Government of India.

What is an India e-Visa?

An India e-Visa is an electronic travel authorization issued by the Government of India that allows eligible foreign nationals to enter India for specific purposes.

The application is completed online, and once approved, the traveler receives the Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) via email before traveling.

Countries Eligible

Citizens of many countries are eligible to apply for an India e-Visa, subject to current Government of India eligibility rules.

Documents Required

Most applicants generally need:

  • Valid passport
  • Passport-size photograph
  • Passport information page
  • Valid email address
  • Payment method
  • Travel information

Additional documents may be required depending on visa category.

Submitting your application well before your intended travel date is recommended.

Reasons an Application May Be Delayed or Refused

Applications may be delayed or refused if:

  • Incorrect passport information
  • Incomplete application
  • Poor-quality document uploads
  • Invalid passport
  • Previous immigration violations
  • Security concerns
  • Missing supporting documents
  • Inconsistent information
