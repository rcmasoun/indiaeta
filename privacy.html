Privacy Policy

**We are not affiliated with the Government of India, the Embassy of India, or any Indian immigration authority.**

By accessing or using our website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are

India e-Visa Online is a private travel assistance service that helps travelers prepare and submit India e-Visa applications.

Final decisions regarding visa approval, rejection, validity, and processing are made solely by the Government of India.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect information that you voluntarily provide as well as certain information collected automatically.

2.1 Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Nationality
  • Passport information
  • Passport issue and expiry dates
  • Passport photograph
  • Personal photograph
  • Residential address
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Emergency contact details (if applicable)
  • Travel information
  • Occupation details (where required)
  • Supporting documents required for your application
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party payment providers)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Operating system
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited
  • Referral website
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Prepare and review your India e-Visa application.
  • Verify the completeness and accuracy of submitted information.
  • Process your application request.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application.
  • Respond to customer support inquiries.
  • Process payments securely.
  • Improve our website and services.
  • Detect and prevent fraud or unauthorized activity.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information only when necessary with:

  • Government authorities responsible for processing visa applications.
  • Trusted third-party service providers assisting with payment processing, website hosting, email communication, document management, or customer support.
  • Legal authorities when required by applicable law, court order, or government request.
  • Professional advisers where necessary to protect our legal rights.

4.2 For Legal Reasons

All third-party providers are required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was shared.

5. Data Security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold. In particular:

  • SSL (Secure Socket Layer) encryption.
  • Secure servers.
  • Restricted employee access.
  • Password-protected systems.
  • Regular security monitoring and updates.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your applicable laws, you may have the right to:

  • Access your personal information.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Request restriction of processing.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.

Any changes will become effective immediately upon publication on this page. The "Effective Date" at the top of this policy will indicate the latest revision.

Your continued use of our website after changes have been posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
