Can I apply from any country?

Yes, if you are a citizen of an eligible country. Your physical location does not prevent you from applying, provided you meet the eligibility requirements.

How early should I apply?

It is recommended to apply several days before travel. Some visa categories allow applications up to 120 days before arrival.

Can I extend my e-Visa?

Most India e-Visas are not extendable or convertible.

Do children need an e-Visa?

Yes. Every traveler, including minors, must have an individual passport and a separate e-Visa application.

Do I need to print my e-Visa?

Yes. Carry a printed copy of your approved Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) when traveling to India.

When do I need to pay?

Payment is required after you complete the online application form and before your application can be processed.

What payment methods do you accept?

Visa Mastercard / Debit Cards / Credit Cards / Stripe

Is my payment secure?

Yes. All payments are processed through secure SSL-encrypted payment gateways to protect your personal and financial information.

Will I receive a payment receipt?

Yes. A payment confirmation and receipt will be sent to your registered email address after your transaction is successful.

Can I get a refund?

Visa processing fees are generally non-refundable once the application has been submitted for processing. Please review our Refund Policy for full details.
